About VerifAI

Built to fight misinformation.

VerifAI is an advanced AI-powered fake news detection system built as part of an AI/ML research project. It combines BERT neural language models with SVM ensemble classifiers to provide forensic-grade analysis of news articles and social media content.

The system uses a 4-layer detection architecture: ML pattern recognition, credibility signal auditing, uncertainty logic scanning, and a final consensus engine — delivering accurate verdicts on whether content is REAL, FAKE, or UNCERTAIN.

Trained on 44,000+ verified articles from multiple datasets, VerifAI achieves 98.2% accuracy on the SVM ensemble and integrates a fine-tuned RoBERTa BERT model for deep semantic understanding.
