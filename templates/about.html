Protecting the
Open Web.

Aletheia is an independent research project dedicated to building AI tools that combat the spread of systemic misinformation and fabricated content.

group

The Team

We are a small team of machine learning engineers and journalists who believe that AI can be used to protect rather than pollute the digital ecosystem.

The platform is designed to offer a verifiable "Veracity Layer" on top of unverified claims across the globe.

target

Our Mission

In an era of hyper-optimized disinformation, Aletheia aims to restore the value of truth. We provide the infrastructure for a more transparent information ecosystem.

Where facts are verifiable and accountability is algorithmic.
